About Happy Oak Painting

At Happy Oak Painting & Handyman Services, we don’t just work in Bernardsville—we care for the homes in our community. From meticulous interior and exterior painting to expert deck restoration and pressure washing, our team brings true craftsmanship to every project.

We believe that honest communication and reliable service are just as important as a flawless finish. When you partner with us, you’re choosing a local team dedicated to protecting and beautifying your biggest investment.

Our Services

Pressure washing">

Pressure Washing

Sidewalks, driveways, patios, and decks can become dangerous and slippery if left dirty. Mold on walls produces unhealthy spores.

We use products that are safe for wood, vinyl, aluminum, or stucco, and also safe for plants and shrubbery around your home.

Interior painting">

Interior Painting

We take care of your interior painting needs from ceiling to floor. We prep all surfaces, repair drywall, fill cracks, and cover stains.

Using high-quality paint (Sherwin Williams or Benjamin Moore), we deliver beautiful, lasting results. We also stain wood floors, trim, doors, and cabinets.

Deck refinishing">

Deck Refinish & Maintenance

Enjoy your deck year-round! We repair, refinish, paint, stain, and maintain wood and composite decks, handrails, stairs, and patio furniture.

Wood decks need regular cleaning and staining; composite decks mostly need cleaning. Let us handle the maintenance for you!
